Common Questions About Environmental Compliance

Get answers to the most common questions about MPCB compliance, ETP/STP installation, and environmental clearances in Maharashtra.

CTE (Consent to Establish) is mandatory permission from the Maharashtra Pollution Control Board (MPCB) required before setting up any industry, factory, or commercial establishment that may pollute the environment. CTO (Consent to Operate) is the operational permit required after construction is complete. Both are legally required under the Water Act 1974 and Air Act 1981. Without valid CTE/CTO, your facility can face shutdown orders, heavy penalties, and legal action. Aaradhya Enviro handles the entire application process — from documentation to MPCB liaison — on your behalf.

An ETP (Effluent Treatment Plant) is designed to treat industrial wastewater — water contaminated with chemicals, heavy metals, oils, or other industrial pollutants. It is mandatory for factories, chemical plants, food processing units, and most manufacturing industries. An STP (Sewage Treatment Plant) treats domestic sewage — wastewater from toilets, kitchens, and bathrooms. It is required for housing societies, commercial complexes, hospitals, and hotels. Many large industries require both. Our team at Aaradhya Enviro assesses your specific effluent characteristics and designs the optimal treatment system for your facility.

The timeline for Environmental Clearance (EC) depends on the project category. Category B projects (handled by SEIAA Maharashtra) typically take 4–6 months including the Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) study, public hearing, and appraisal committee review. Category A projects (handled by MoEFCC) can take 6–12 months. However, delays are common without proper documentation. Aaradhya Enviro has extensive experience navigating the EC process and can significantly reduce delays by ensuring all documents are correctly prepared and submitted from day one.

ZLD (Zero Liquid Discharge) is an advanced water treatment approach where all industrial wastewater is treated and recycled — leaving zero liquid waste discharged to the environment. MPCB and the National Green Tribunal (NGT) have made ZLD mandatory for several highly polluting industries including textile dyeing units, tanneries, sugar factories, paper mills, and pharmaceutical plants. Even if your industry is not currently mandated, implementing ZLD can dramatically reduce water consumption and operational costs. Aaradhya Enviro designs, installs, and operates complete ZLD systems tailored to your effluent volume and composition.

The cost of an ETP or STP installation varies widely depending on your effluent volume (KLD — kilolitres per day), the nature of pollutants, and the treatment technology required. A small ETP for 10–50 KLD can start from ₹5–15 lakhs, while larger systems for 200+ KLD industrial units can go up to ₹1 crore or more. Aaradhya Enviro offers free site assessments and provides detailed project cost estimates with no obligation.
