1. About Us

Environmental pollution is one of the most pressing challenges facing industries today — threatening the quality of the air we breathe, the water we drink, and the food we grow. As regulations tighten, industries face increasing pressure to operate responsibly while remaining profitable.

Aaradhya Enviro was founded to solve exactly this problem. We are a premier environmental consultancy organization based in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar (Aurangabad), Maharashtra, dedicated to helping industries strike the right balance between operational efficiency and environmental responsibility.

Over the last 15+ years, we have grown from a local compliance consultancy into a full-spectrum environmental solutions provider, now serving clients PAN India. Our team has successfully delivered 500+ projects for clients ranging from small manufacturing units to large industrial groups, sugar factories, construction companies, and multinational corporations.

What sets us apart is that we don't just help you get paperwork approved — we design, install, operate, and maintain the actual physical systems (ETPs, STPs, RO plants, air pollution control equipment) that keep your facility compliant every single day.

This means you get one single, accountable partner for everything related to environment, health, and safety (EHS) — instead of juggling multiple vendors, consultants, and contractors.

4. Legal & Regulatory Compliance

Services
01

Legal Compliances (MPCB Consents)

Scope: CTE (Consent to Establish) and CTO (Consent to Operate) filings and representation.

Process: We assess category (Red/Orange/Green), prepare documentation, file on the portal, and handle MPCB liaison.

Who Needs It

Any facility producing effluent, air emissions, or hazardous waste in Maharashtra.

02

Environmental Clearances (EC)

Scope: End-to-end approvals from state committees (SEIAA) and central ministry (MoEFCC).

Deliverables: Baseline monitoring, Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) reports, and public hearing coordination.

Who Needs It

New setups or expansion of mining, infrastructure, and large industrial facilities.

03 Audit & Regulatory Reporting

Timely filing of mandatory returns is crucial to prevent compliance penalization.

  • Form IV (Hazardous Waste Returns)
  • Form V (Environmental Statement)
  • Environmental audits & compliance

04 CGWA & Bio-Medical NOCs

Specialized approvals for groundwater use and clinical waste management.

  • Ground water extraction permissions
  • Bio-Medical waste authorization
  • MPCB/CPCB portal representation

5. Turnkey Projects

Engineering

We deliver complete, end-to-end design, fabrication, installation, and commissioning under a single contract. By acting as the sole point of accountability, we eliminate common multi-vendor delays.

ETP & STP Installation

Custom-designed Effluent Treatment Plants (ETP) for chemical/industrial wastewater, and Sewage Treatment Plants (STP) for domestic sewage.

1. Characterize ➔ 2. Design ➔ 3. Install

ZLD & RO Systems

Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) and Reverse Osmosis (RO) systems to recycle 100% of industrial wastewater, achieving maximum water recovery.

  • High recovery RO filtration
  • MEE & MVRE thermal systems

Air Pollution Control

Tailored air pollution control devices designed to treat flue gas and process emissions to match strict emission parameters.

  • Packed column wet scrubbers
  • Pulse-jet baghouse filters

Plant Up-gradation

Evaluation and retrofitting of existing under-performing treatment plants to improve capacity and effluent standards.

  • Detailed capacity gap analysis
  • Minimal plant shutdown downtime

6. Monitoring & Management

Operations

Air, Water & Soil Testing

Scheduled environmental sampling and laboratory analyses to track plant compliance levels.

  • • Stack emission and flue gas monitoring
  • • Raw and treated wastewater characterization
  • • Boundary ambient noise monitoring studies

Online Monitoring (OCEMS)

Installation and data linkage of Continuous Emission/Effluent Quality Monitoring Systems.

  • • CEMS and CEQMS real-time sensors
  • • Live connectivity with MPCB & CPCB servers
  • • Routine calibration and protocol audits

Wastewater Treatability Studies

Laboratory scale simulation trials to verify chemical feasibility of proposed treatment trains.

  • • Aerobic & anaerobic digestion trials
  • • Coagulation-flocculation optimization
  • • COD/refractory organics reduction studies

Hazardous Waste & EPR

Management support for hazardous chemical sludge and Plastic packaging Extended Producer Responsibility.

  • • Form 10 hazardous manifest tracking
  • • EPR registration and annual filings on CPCB portal
  • • Safe industrial waste storage & disposal coordination

Operations, Maintenance (O&M) & Training

Installing treatment systems is only the first step. Consistent operations are essential to maintain compliance limits. We provide complete chemical dosing, daily logging, and EHS training for plant personnel.
